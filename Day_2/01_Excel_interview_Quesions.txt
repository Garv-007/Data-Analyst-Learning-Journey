wildcard

🎤 Interview Questions
Q1

What is a wildcard?
- wild card is a symbols like * and ? which is used in excel for finding the exact matches from a cell

Q2

Difference between * and ?
- * is used to find exact full match after star function and ? is used to find single match after ? mark.


Q3

What does

"Sales*"

mean?  

- in this * will start searching the letters after sales and give every name whose starts with sales.

Q4

What does

"*Sales"

mean?

- in this * will start searching the letters before  sales and give every name whose ends with sales.


Q5

What does

"*Sales*"

mean?

- It will search the sales from start and also search from end and give output where sales letter were seen.
